About SPIDERMAN:

Peter Parker, a Queens teenager, gains spider-like powers after a radioactive spider bite. Initially seeking fame, tragedy strikes when his Uncle Ben is killed by a thief he chose not to stop. Motivated by Uncle Ben's words, Peter becomes Spider-Man and embraces responsibility. His life takes a tragic turn when Gwen Stacy is killed by the Green Goblin. Grieving, Peter eventually forms a relationship with Mary Jane Watson. The black costume, obtained during the "Secret Wars" event, turns out to be an alien symbiont, leading to the creation of Venom with Eddie Brock. Peter's powers are linked to Native American totemic forces, and he faces challenges like the formidable Morlun. Tony Stark mentors Peter, providing a new suit amid the Superhuman Registration Act controversy. Peter initially supports the act but later defects to Captain America's side, leading to his current fugitive status.
